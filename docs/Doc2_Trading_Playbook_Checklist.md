## THE STACKING PROGRESSION

Read top-to-bottom as the trading day unfolds. Each step adds edge and justifies adding position.

### STEP 1: ORB20 LOCKS (9:50 AM ET)

**Check:** Did price close outside the ORB20 range?

**Baseline probability:** 68.8% day closes in breakout direction

| Factor to Check | Bull Break | Bear Break |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| ORB close near high (>80%) → bull break | 66.5% 1x win | — |
| ORB close near low (<20%) → bear break | — | 66.1% 1x win |
| ORB close MIDDLE (40-60%) → any break | 63.0% | 56.0% ⚠️ |
| ORB close OPPOSED to break | 62.8% | 63.1% |

**Action:** Enter HALF position if aligned. Enter QUARTER if neutral. Skip if middle-close bear.

**Example:**
> ORB20 forms 9:30-9:50. High = 5525.40, Low = 5518.20, Range = 7.20 pts.
> ORB closes at 5524.80 (close position = 91% — near the high).
> At 9:50, price closes at 5526.10 — above ORB high. **Bull breakout.**
> ORB close aligned with bull break → 66.5% base win rate.
> **Enter HALF position (bull credit spread or directional).**

## PROGRESSION EXAMPLE — FULL DAY

**Setup:** Tuesday, January 14, 2025. SPX at 5920. ATR14 = 65 pts.

**9:30-9:50 AM — ORB20 forms:**
- High: 5928.40, Low: 5919.80, Range: 8.60 pts
- ORB/ATR: 8.60/65 = 13.2% (small — good, +2 pts)
- ORB closes at 5927.90 (close pct = 94% — near high)

**9:50 AM — ORB20 breaks bull:**
- Close at 5929.10 (above 5928.40)
- Immediate breakout (+1)
- ORB close aligned with bull (+1)
- Tuesday (+0), Buffer = 5928.40 - 5925 = $3.40 (+0)
- **Base grade: +4 = DOUBLE tier (67.6% 1x win, 83.4% 0.5x)**
- **Enter HALF position** (waiting for confirmation stack)
- Day favorable probability: 68.8%

**10:00 AM — ORB30 confirms bull:**
- ORB30 range: 5930.10 - 5919.80 = 10.30 pts
- ORB30 close near high
- **Add to NORMAL. Day favorable: 72.2%**

**10:30 AM — ORB60 confirms bull:**
- ORB60 range: 5934.20 - 5918.50 = 15.70 pts
- OR60/OR20 ratio: 15.70/8.60 = 1.83x (good expansion)
- ORB60 close near high
- All 3 same + all 3 aligned
- **Add to PLUS → DOUBLE. Day favorable: 90.9%. Past ORB20 high at close: 77.0%**

**Credit spread placement:**
- Short strike at ORB20 midpoint (5924.10 → nearest $5 = 5925)
- 82.7% probability of being OTM at close
- $5 wide spread, collecting ~$2.40

**2:00 PM — Exit check:**
- Price at 5941. Well above ORB levels. Time decay has eroded option premium.
- Close credit spread for ~80% of max profit.

**4:00 PM — Day closes at 5938.50:**
- Above open ✓, Above ORB20 high ✓, Above ORB20 midpoint ✓
- Trade successful on all metrics.
